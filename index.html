Campus Life

Pine View School

It was established in 1970

Pine View School for the Gifted, or simply known as Pine View School, is a public, college-preparatory, coeducational school located in Osprey, Florida. Pine View serves students from 2nd through 12th grades.


The Quad

Outdoor are for passing periods

The Quad is a new area with awnings for rain, and it has picnic tables to eat lunch in. It connects to the other parts of campus and is occupied by students for passing periods.

Achievements

Best Public School in Florida
Best Public School- 2nd to 12th Grade

Pine View is the 3rd best public school in the USA as well! In the same year, Pine View was ranked by Newsweek as the 4th best high school in Florida. U.S. News & World Report ranked Pine View the best high school in Florida, based on its 2013 national ranking. In 2009, Pine View was rated America's Best High School for Florida by Business Week.

2015 Green Ribbon Schools Award
Award for sustainability

Pine View conserves energy in many ways. The school is powered by solar panels. We pratice conserving resources and energy. Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) courses and hands-on learning are an integral part of Pine View. Students participate in hands-on labs, science fairs, and outdoor classroom science field trips to state parks. Students also learn respect and appreciation of our environment through art and music.

What's New

  • New Building 17

    The new building is the location of many new classes. It is 3 floors high, equipped with an elevator as well as new facilities, like a College and Career center, and spacious classrooms.

  • Mourning Ethan

    A rock garden has been built to honor a student named Ethan who died in a sailing accident. His interest in geology sparked an idea for this rock garden, and it is filled with his memories.
